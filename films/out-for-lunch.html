Film Summary
A short film blending jazz soundtrack and spoken word poetic narration, examining themes of class privilege, drug use, and masculinity within student communities.
My Role
I was co-writer and director on Out for Lunch. Additionally, I was responsible for camerawork and sound during production, as we used a single-camera setup, and was editor on the project, carrying out video editing, sound mixing, and colour grading.
