Film Summary
A teenage amateur boxer experiences a moral crisis when a relationship with a teaching assistant results in a pregnancy.
My Role
A no-budget short made with the help of friends and family, I was scriptwriter, director and producer on Skin & Bones. During filming, I was one of two crew members, and was responsible for camerawork and sound recording in addition to my directorial duties. I served as sole editor on the project, and was responsible for all video editing, sound mixing, and colour grading.
