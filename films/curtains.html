Curtains

Short Film (4 mins)
Director/Writer/Editor
Film Summary
An ageing man drifts through disintegrating memories of his past.
My Role
A no-budget short made during COVID-19 lockdown, based on a story I wrote and recorded earlier in the year. Made using a combination of footage from my archive and some new footage shot with limited equipment. A personal project, I was the sole crew member for Curtains, responsible for camera, sound, and editing.
