This Must Be the Verse

Short Documentary (8 mins)
Director/Camera/Editor
Film Summary
My dissertation documentary for Newcastle University's Film Practices BA. Filmed under COVID-19 lockdown conditions, the film uses archival family photos and candid interviews with my parents in which they reflect on their experiences raising a family, and, soon, their own childhoods, deconstructing the maternal and paternal figures and revealing loss, regret, and vulnerability beneath the parental roles. Graded 1st class.
My Role
I was the sole filmmaker & crew member on the project, and was responsible for concept, interviews, visuals, sound, and editing.
